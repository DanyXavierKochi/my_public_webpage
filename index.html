About St. Anthony’s Church, Kannamali

St. Anthony’s Church in the village of Kannamali (near Kochi, Kerala, India) is a beloved place of worship with a strong community tradition. Its scenic coastal location adds to the quiet beauty and spiritual atmosphere.

The church is known for its warm welcome, regular masses and community events — including annual feasts that draw both locals and visitors. The architecture reflects a blend of Kerala‑style simplicity and liturgical elegance.

Visitors often appreciate the serene setting by the sea, the friendly parish community, and the soothing environment for reflection and prayer.
